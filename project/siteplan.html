Website Planning Document

Site Name: Malawi Delicacies

Malawian Delicacies represents the rich culinary heritage of Malawi, focusing on traditional recipes and cultural significance

Site Purpose

The Local Malawian Cuisine website aims to be a comprehensive resource for exploring traditional Malawian dishes. It will provide recipes, information about ingredients, cultural insights, and a guide to local restaurants. The site will also feature a contact form for visitors to get in touch with questions or feedback.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What are the most popular traditional Malawian dishes?
  • Scenario 2: Where can I find authentic Malawian ingredients in my area?
  • Scenario 3: How can I contact the site administrator for more information?

Color Schema

Secondary Color: #ecb62c - Used for secondary elements like links, accent elements and buttons

Tertiary Color: #f9f9f9 - Used for background sections to create contrast

Typography

Secondary Font: Barlow Condensed - Used for headings and subheadings.
